What to Expect

Meaningful Conversation

We'll discuss your story, experiences, and what elements are most important to capture in your visual narrative.

Creative Brainstorming

Together, we'll explore artistic concepts, visual themes, and possible approaches to representing your story.

Project Planning

We'll discuss timeline, investment, and create a roadmap for how your project will unfold from concept to completion.

Q&A Time

Bring your questions! This is your opportunity to learn more about the process and ensure Story Ink is the right fit for your vision.

After our consultation, you'll receive a follow-up email with a summary of our discussion and a personalized project proposal.
