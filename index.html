My Story

Hi! I'm Amy! As an artist, writer, blogger, traveler, teacher, and storyteller, I've always been captivated by the power of narrative. Stories shape our perspectives, deepen our empathy, and strengthen our connections. They help us understand where we've been and who we are. As an artist, I wanted to express them in a less traditional way.

With Story Ink, I set out to capture these personal narratives in a unique, hand-drawn visual form. My Process

From concept to creation: how your story becomes art

1

Consultation

We'll discuss your story, share ideas, and explore how to best visually represent your narrative. This can be done virtually or in person.

2

Concept Development

I'll create preliminary sketches and concept designs based on our consultation, incorporating elements that are meaningful to your story.

3

Feedback & Refinement

You'll review the initial concepts and provide feedback. We'll refine the design together until it perfectly captures your vision.

4

Artistic Creation

I'll hand-draw your visual story with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every element represents a piece of your narrative.

5

Final Delivery

Your completed visual story is professionally packaged and delivered to you, ready to be displayed and treasured for years to come.
